Who am I?

I'm a Canadian user interface designer and illustrator currently living in Ottawa, Ontario. I'm a strong believer in clean, smart and functional design. My goal is to make life easier for individuals to gain experience and information through digital and print sources. With that being said, I have a keen eye for pixel perfect detail and visually appealing content placement. Aside from all that, I'm an enthusiast in street photography and kick ass at Dance Dance Revolution.
