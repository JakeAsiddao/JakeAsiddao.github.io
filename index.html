PLANET INK IDENTITY REBRAND

PLANET INK IDENTITY REBRAND

The objective of this project was to take an existing small Canadian business with poor branding and completely rebrand with a fresh new logo.

The overall key message of this logo is showcase to viewers that this is a professional tattoo establishment that takes time and care in their procedures. Showing that they are sophisticated but still hold that classic tattoo parlor atmosphere. From the logo it is identified that elements from traditional style tattoos are used in the design, therefore showcasing that style as one of their specialties. The modern redesign also shows viewers that this place is up to date and gives a new visual outlook on Ottawa based tattoo parlours.

IZZE AD CAMPAIGN

IZZE AD CAMPAIGN

The objective was to create an ad campaign design that demonstrates creativity and the ability to stay within campaign restraints. The client chosen for this project was Izze, a sparkling juice company. This client was chosen because it appeals the most to the younger/modern generation. This was taken into consideration while designing the concepts.

The slogan for this campaign is Natural Unveiled. Since the drink is made up of 70% natural flavoring, the designs were based on combining nature itself with the drink. With this in mind, three elements of nature were used: Grass, water, and trees. The choice of elements were carefully thought out to keep a variety in the colors, textures and approach. To follow the overall concept of the slogan, each bottle was designed to appear as if they are being uncovered or emerging out of the elements.

www.izze.com

who the hell am I?

I'm a Canadian Designer currently living in Ottawa, Ontario. I'm a strong believer in clean and simple design. I like to ask myself, "What am I trying to prove" when pushing a concept forward, then use my design to back up my answer. My primary goal is solving the problem, second goal, make it visually flawless. My main strengths in design include Branding, Print and User Interface design. Aside from all that, I'm an enthusiast in street photography and kick ass at Dance Dance Revolution.
