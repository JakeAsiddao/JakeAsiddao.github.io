who the hell am I?

I'm a Canadian Designer currently living in Ottawa, Ontario. I'm a strong believer in clean and simple design. I like to ask myself, "What am I trying to prove" when pushing a concept forward, then use my design to back up my answer. My primary goal is solving the problem, second goal, make it visually flawless and BLAH BLAH BLAH BLAH BLAH... Aside from all that, I'm an enthusiast in street photography and kick ass at Dance Dance Revolution.
